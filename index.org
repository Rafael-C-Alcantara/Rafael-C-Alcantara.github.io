* About
I am a postdoctoral fellow at the the University of Texas at
Austin. I have recently graduated as a PhD in Business
Economics at [[https://www.insper.edu.br/en/][Insper,]] where I worked under the supervision of
[[https://hedibert.org/][Dr. Hedibert Lopes]]. For the first semester of 2023, I was a
visiting scholar at the [[https://math.asu.edu/][School of Mathematical and
Statistical Sciences]] at Arizona State University working
with [[https://math.la.asu.edu/~prhahn/][Dr. Richard Hahn]].
